Venue Information

Donington Park Farmhouse

The venue we have chosen for our wedding is Donington Park Farmhouse, a 17th century farmhouse restored to accomadate weddings, parties and events.

We look forward to getting married in the either the Hitching Room or the Threshing Barn, eating in the Pigsties, then dancing(maybe) and drinking(plenty) into the evening.

For more information please click the link to their own website belew the images.

Accommodation

Donington Park Farmhouse has 19 rooms available to pre-book should you wish. Again, use the link above to book through their website.

Directions

The venue is easy to find. From the A453 at Isley Walton, take the Melbourne turning. It's half a mile along this road on the right; you can't miss the beautiful black and white farmhouse. I have added a google maps with the venue already loaded in the link.
